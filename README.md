# YRC Worldwide (yrc-worldwide)

YRC Worldwide (rebranded as Yellow Corporation in 2021) was a holding company whose subsidiaries — YRC Freight, Holland, Reddaway, New Penn, and HNRY Logistics — provided less-than-truckload (LTL) freight transportation, brokerage, and logistics services across North America. The company ceased operations on July 30, 2023 and filed for Chapter 11 bankruptcy on August 6, 2023, ending nearly 100 years of operation. Bankruptcy asset sales redistributed terminals and equipment to Saia, XPO, Estes, and other carriers; no public developer APIs were ever published.

## Features

| Name | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Less-Than-Truckload Shipping | National and regional LTL freight transportation across the U.S., Canada, and Mexico. |
| Shipment Tracking | PRO number, bill of lading, PO number, booking number, and load number tracking via the customer portals. |
| Rate Quoting | Online rate quote tools for obtaining freight pricing prior to shipment. |
| Bill of Lading Management | Web-based creation and management of bills of lading and shipping documents. |
| Claims Management | Cargo claim filing, overcharge claims, and claim status tracking. |
| Invoicing and Online Payment | Open invoice management, online payment, and payment history lookup by check or PRO. |

## Use Cases

| Name | Description |
|------|-------------|
| National LTL Distribution | Long-haul less-than-truckload movement between major U.S. metro areas via YRC Freight. |
| Regional LTL Service | Next-day and two-day regional LTL coverage through Holland (Midwest), Reddaway (West), and New Penn (Northeast). |
| Cross-Border Freight | U.S.–Canada and U.S.–Mexico LTL service through YRC Freight's North American network. |
| Freight Brokerage | Truckload brokerage and managed transportation through HNRY Logistics. |
